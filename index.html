About

Film Finder was launched by Amit Kumar in 2007, as a Patna-based film website that also offered social media consulting services to film studios. Using an open-posting model that invites readers to write their own content and a recommendation algorithm for movies, Film Finder grew to more than 6.8 million unique monthly views by October 2014 (Google Analytics).
Since its launch Film Finder has expanded into other publishing verticals.
